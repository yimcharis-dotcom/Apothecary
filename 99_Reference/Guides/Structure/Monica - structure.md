### 2. **Set Up Folder Structure**

- **Practical Tips**:
    - **Hierarchical Organization**:
        - Create a clear hierarchy to reflect your diverse interests. Here’s a suggested structure:
            
            ```
            /Personal Wiki
            ├── /Projects
            │   ├── /AI
            │   ├── /Audit
            │   └── /Accounting
            ├── /Learning
            │   ├── /AI Concepts
            │   ├── /Accounting Principles
            │   └── /Audit Techniques
            ├── /Prompts Library
            ├── /Reviews
            └── /Resources
            ```
            
    - **Descriptive Folder Names**:
        - Use clear, descriptive names for folders to easily identify content. Avoid abbreviations unless they are widely understood.
    - **Color-Coding**:
        - If using Obsidian themes, consider color-coding folders or notes to visually differentiate between areas. For example, use blue for AI-related notes and green for accounting.
